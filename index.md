# Privacy Policy for ClearLedger

ClearLedger is a manual finance planning application designed to give users full control over their financial data.

## Data Collection
ClearLedger does not collect, transmit, or store personal or financial data on external servers. All information entered into the app is stored locally on the user's device.

## Bank Accounts and Financial Institutions
ClearLedger does not connect to bank accounts, credit card providers, or financial institutions. All data is entered manually by the user.

## Data Sharing
ClearLedger does not share user data with third parties.

## Data Security
Because ClearLedger operates entirely with manual data entry, sensitive financial information is not transmitted off the device.
